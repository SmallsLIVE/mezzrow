  1. Tickets are for our Listening Room. Tickets are not necessary for our lounge.

  3. We cannot guarantee that parties larger than 3 will be able to sit together.
  4. Your ticket is good for the one-set that you have chosen. Sets will be sold individually. If there are available seats and you wish to stay for another set, the management will invite you.

  6. Your name will be on a list at the door when you arrive. The manger will confirm your reservation and show you to your seat.
  7. Tickets can be refunded in person at the club up until the day before the show. Please notify us by email (mezzrowclub@gmail.com) if you need to cancel your ticket for a refund.
  8. Late-comers will be seated at the discretion of the management.
  9. The will be no “paper” ticket – just a confirmation number that you will receive via email.
