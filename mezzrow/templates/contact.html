Mezzrow policies

MEZZROW is a place for adults to convene. We ask that you please dress smartly. In our lounge area adult behavior is expected and polite conversation acceptable. In our listening room we expect respectful behavior towards the artists who are performing and for the other people who are sharing the music with you. In our listening room we ask that you keep conversation to a minimum.

Please be aware that Mezzrow is in the basement of a residential building. We are here by the good graces of our neighbors and have promised that their quality of life will not be compromised by our business or cliental. Please enter and exit the club quietly, particularly later in the evening when tenants may be asleep.

There is no smoking allowed in Mezzrow. If you wish to smoke a cigarette, we ask that you exit the club and cross the street to the garage side of 10th street so as not to disturb our neighbors. If you need a taxi, we suggest that you hail one directly on 7th avenue at the corner of 10th street. This is also an excellent location to be let off if you are arriving in one.

Mezzrow has the right, at the discretion of the management, to remove a client from the premises if they behave in a manner that is unruly, disruptive or offensive.

Mezzrow is a place dedicated to jazz music and the culture of Greenwich Village and New York City.
